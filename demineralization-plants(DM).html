Product Overview

Demineralization (DM) Plants are advanced water treatment systems designed to remove mineral salts and ions from water, producing high-purity demineralized water. These plants are essential for industries requiring ultra-pure water for various processes.

Our DM plants utilize ion exchange technology with cation and anion exchange resins to effectively remove cations and anions, resulting in water with very low conductivity and high purity levels.

Why Demineralization Plants Are Important?

Demineralized water is crucial for numerous industrial applications where mineral-free water is essential to prevent scaling, corrosion, and product contamination. DM plants ensure consistent water quality for critical processes.

  • Prevents scaling in boilers, cooling towers, and heat exchangers
  • Eliminates corrosion caused by dissolved minerals
  • Ensures product quality in pharmaceutical and food industries
  • Improves efficiency and extends equipment lifespan
