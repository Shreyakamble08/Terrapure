About Terrapure

Since 2014, Terrapure Environment Services Pvt. Ltd.(formerly Earth Environment Services) has been a trusted name in water and wastewater treatment. We deliver ETPs, STPs, WTPs, RO systems, ZLD, and recycling technologies that keep industries compliant, communities healthy, and the environment safe.

Our mission is to provide sustainable, technology-driven solutions that reduce waste, recycle water, and move industries closer to zero liquid discharge.

Led by Mr. Dhairyashil Gaud , with 35+ years of engineering experience, our team is committed to innovation, precision, and sustainability.
