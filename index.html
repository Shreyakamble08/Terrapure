About Terrapure

Since 2014, Terrapure Environment Services has been a trusted name in water and wastewater treatment, delivering ETPs, STPs, WTPs, RO systems, ZLD, and recycling technologies that keep industries compliant and the environment safe.

Our mission is to provide sustainable, technology-driven solutions that reduce waste, recycle water, and move industries closer to zero liquid discharge.
