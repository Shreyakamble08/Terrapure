Product Overview

Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) is an advanced water treatment process that eliminates liquid waste discharge from industrial plants. Our ZLD systems are designed to recover maximum water for reuse while converting dissolved solids into dry, disposable solids.

The ZLD process involves multiple stages of treatment including pretreatment, reverse osmosis, evaporation, and crystallization to achieve complete water recovery. This technology is essential for industries facing strict environmental regulations and water scarcity challenges.

Why Zero Liquid Discharge Plants Are Important?

ZLD technology addresses critical environmental and regulatory challenges faced by industries. It ensures complete compliance with pollution control norms while promoting sustainable water management practices.

  • Eliminates industrial wastewater discharge completely
  • Maximizes water recovery and reuse up to 95-99%
  • Ensures compliance with stringent environmental regulations
  • Reduces freshwater consumption and operational costs
