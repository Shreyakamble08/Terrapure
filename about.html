About Terrapure

Leading the way in sustainable environmental solutions with innovative water treatment technologies

Company Overview

Terrapure Environment Services Pvt. Ltd., formerly Earth Environment Services (est. 2014), is a trusted name in water and wastewater treatment. We deliver large-scale, reliable, and sustainable solutions for industries and communities.

With 10+ years of proven expertise and projects across diverse sectors, we specialize in ETPs, STPs, WTPs, RO systems, and advanced recycling technologies.

Guided by Mr. Dhairyashil Gaud, our Managing Director with 35+ years of engineering leadership, we combine innovation with sustainability to build a cleaner future.
