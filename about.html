Company Overview

Terrapure Environment Services Pvt. Ltd., formerly Earth Environment Services (est. 2014), is a leading manufacturer and service provider of advanced water and wastewater treatment solutions tailored for industrial and municipal requirements.

We specialize in design, engineering, manufacturing, installation, and end-to-end project execution with a strong focus on innovation, quality control, and sustainability to meet global standards.

With over 35+ years of engineering leadership, our in-house team ensures seamless project delivery, efficient plant performance, and long-term operational reliability across diverse sectors.
